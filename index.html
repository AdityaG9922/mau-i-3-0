About Mauli

Crafting Excellence
Since 2016

Mauli Kitchen Trolly & Fabricators is a trusted name in Pune for high-quality kitchen trolleys and custom fabrication work. Based in Dhayri, we bring modern design and sturdy craftsmanship together for every home and business we serve.

From sleek kitchen trolleys with modern finishing to robust gates, grilles, and furniture frames — our work speaks through every weld, every finish, and every satisfied customer who recommends us.
